Centrifugal Labs LTD is a Cyprus-registered software company (ΗΕ 446709), founded in 2023 by the creators of Centrifugo. We are a small, focused team specializing in real-time applications, with a mission to deliver a robust, self-hosted real-time messaging platform designed to scale reliably in production environments.

We are based in Limassol, in the heart of Cyprus. Limassol is known for its rich history, stunning beaches, and vibrant culture. The city is a hub for technology and innovation, making it an ideal location for our company.
